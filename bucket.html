Bucket

Model, Competitive Eater, Professional Napper

Bucket, or Bucky for short, is a 2 year old orange tabby cat who has the utmost sense of curiosity and adventure. He was adopted by Lilly and JP on December 2021 and has been living at the Mitra estate ever since. Despite his large stature, Bucky has a gentle heart and meows to welcome friendly guests into his domain. His social battery varies between morning and night, but his drive for attention persists constantly throughout the day regardless of whatever activity his other housemates are doing at the time. His random bursts of energy, running laps among multiple rooms to hunt are shadowed by the time he spends in the day napping in different locations of the apartment. One of Bucky's favorite spots to nap, the foot of the bed with a fuzzy blanket, is the best place to catch a cuddle session with him as he prefers to snooze here around mid to late afternoon when he is the most snuggly. His other favorite spot to nap is inside the laundry basket, currently renamed as Bucket's bucket.

In his off time, Bucky enjoys walking around the apartment complex with Lilly and JP, eating dirt and grass, chasing strings and feathers, hunting flies (karate-chop), and sitting on flat surfaces to relax. He likes sniffing shoes and uses a particularly stinky pair as "practice prey" during his night time hunts. As a part of his occassional ritual, he leaves his practice prey in front of Lilly's and JP's bedroom door as a token of appreciation for a successful night's hunt. He also stoically stares outside balconies or windows to purview the neighborhood cat gangs and contemplates on joining them someday.
